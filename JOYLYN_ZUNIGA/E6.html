My Favorite Song

My favorite as of now is, "Love to Hate Me" by BLACKPINK. A side-track from their 1st ever album, "The Album"

The song seemingly talks about how the group has seen so many envious people grow around them as they skyrocketed into fame and success all over the world.

But aside from that, one of the reasons why I love the song is because its sending out a clear message saying that we shouldn't care about any negativity and hate in our lives.
